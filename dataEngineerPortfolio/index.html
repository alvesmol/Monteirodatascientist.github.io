Manchine Learning Models
To understand Customer Churn

In this logistic regression analysis for a telecom company, we uncovered crucial insights for boosting customer retention: Customer Value vs. Churn: Higher customer value links to lower churn. Focusing on enhancing customer value is key. Subscription Length & Loyalty: Longer subscriptions tend to mean lower churn. Strategies to reward long-term loyalty are beneficial. Usage Frequency: More frequent service use (calls, SMS) shows varied churn rates. Tailoring services to usage patterns can be effective. Demographics & Plans: Churn differs across age groups and tariff plans. Customized strategies for these segments can improve satisfaction and loyalty. Service Quality: Call failures and complaints greatly influence churn. Prioritizing service quality and quick complaint resolution is essential. These insights guide our approach to reducing churn and elevating customer engagement in the telecom sector, showcasing a comprehensive yet concise overview of our project.
